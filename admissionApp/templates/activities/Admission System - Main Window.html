Admission

ADMISSIONS AT TUP

The Office of Admissions assists students to obtain a realistic picture of their abilities, aptitudes, interests, personality characteristics and school achievements. The objectives are as follows: (1) to enable the student's realize the full extent of their educational potentials; (2) to identify student's strengths and weaknesses; and (3) to know the implication of these, thus, help the counselor have a better picture of the students.
Other Announcements
The Admissions Office has already started accepting freshmen applicants for School Year 2019-2020. Interested applicants may download the TUP Application Form for College Admission Test available in this web site and accomplish this and present together with the requirements to the Admissions Office from Monday to Friday, 8:00am to 5:00pm. The admission test is given every third Sunday of the month and the result is released three weeks after.

ADMISSION PROCEDURES

  1. Applicants must apply online at www.tup.edu.ph
  2. Click Admissions and select ONLINE APPLICATION.
  3. Once directed to APPLICANTS ACCESS MODULE, click Apply.
  4. Fill out Profile Verification, choose Cavite Campus and click CONTINUE.
  5. Provide necessary information from Step 1 to Step 4 then click Submit.
  6. Proceed to TUPC - Admissions Office for evaluation and submission of Application Requirements:
    • Hard copy of accomplished application form;
    • Two (2) recent 2"x2" pictures on plain white background;
    • For Freshmen, photocopy of Grade 12 Senior High School card;
    • For Transferee, photocopy of Transcript of Records (TOR); and,
    • There should be NO FAILING GRADES reflected either in the card or TOR.
  7. Pay appropriate fees at the Cashier's Office.
  8. Return to TUPC - Admissions Office for test permit issuance.

ENROLLMENT REQUIREMENTS

  1. Scores of the applicant met the required cut-off scores set for the program choice
  2. Applicant is qualified for interview as an additional requirement for the program of choice.
  3. Results of the Medical Examination showed that the applicant is physically fit.
  4. Submission of the original copies of the following:
    • For Freshmen, FORM 138(High School Card)
    • For Transferees, Transcript of Records and Honorable Dismissal
    • Barangay Clearance
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  5. Submission of photocopy of PSA/NSO Birth Certificate
